Sonic games exceed 1.5 billion in total sales and downloads

SEGA shared with us a detailed financial report on sales of its biggest titles, and as expected, the Sonic the Hedgehog series took the lead with over 1.5 billion copies sold and different downloads from the web.

To put things into perspective, this number includes sales of various series games, as well as downloads of many of the free Sonic titles that have been released over the years, and while the series sold well at its peak in the 1990s, the explosion of the mobile game market has moved The series games to new heights with a huge fan base.

Since 2013, Sega has released Sonic Dash, Sonic Runners, Sonic Dash 2: Sonic Boom, and Sonic Runners Adventures on smartphones, and the series' most popular game, Sonic Dash, is said to have surpassed 500 million downloads as of 2021, making up Over a third of all series sales advertised by SEGA.

Sonic Forces: Speed Battle, a mobile game from 2017, is also said to have surpassed 94 million downloads as of April 2022.

While Sega rarely releases any firm data about sales of premium games for the series, last year it claimed that during its 2021 fiscal year, Mario and Sonic at the Olympic Games in Tokyo 2020, Team Sonic Racing, Sonic Mania, and Sonic Generations sold more of 4.4 million units.
